Books 4 Boredom

Building a community that loves to read books
Hanga i tētahi hapori e aroha ana ki te pānui pukapuka

In 2019, Books 4 Boredom was set up by a number of Year 12 students from Mountain View High School who were concerned with the drop in numbers of students who read books. They decided to create a group which encouraged students to read more. The group promotes books that students might enjoy reading, and also organizes public book readings and signing events.

Books can be an escape from the daily pressures of life; a way to exercise your imagination and mental concentration. The joy of reading also has many health benefits associated with it. The students felt that the decline in students reading habits needed to change, many students did not like going to the library (it wasn’t cool), so the book reading community idea was launched.
